How it works

Three steps. No team-building. No volume thresholds.

Refer · install · get paid. The boring version of "passive income."

01refer

Refer or accept the assignment

Send a customer your tracking link (Path 1 — hustler) using your custom subdomain yourname.smashone.us. Or accept a by-request assignment when SmashOne or a partner brings you a client (Path 3/4). Or distribute your referral link for clients who self-setup (Path 2b — no setup work).

02setup

You set up the customer

Customer signs up Day 1 — $0 charged during the 14-day free trial. You spend 1–2 hours configuring their trio (Facebook + Instagram + Telegram OAuth + content calendar + AI-assistant tuning). On Day 15 the customer pays one combined transaction: $79 subscription + $199 setup fee = $278 total (or $79 alone for Path 2b — no setup work, no setup fee).

03paid

You get paid

Day 30 release (after the 14-day money-back window closes): $139.30 if you found the client (Path 1, 70% × $199) or $99.50 if it was by-request (Path 3/4, 50% × $199). Plus $7.90/month per active customer (10% L1) + $3.95/month per L2 sub-referral — paid lifetime, paused if customer cancels. Paid monthly via Stripe Connect Express. No clawbacks past Day 30.

That's it. We do the rest.
