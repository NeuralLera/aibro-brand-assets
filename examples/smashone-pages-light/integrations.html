Why exactly six

Why we ship six platforms, not twenty.

We watched competitors stretch to 20+ platforms and saw owners use only 3–4. The integration tax (rate limits, broken auth refresh, API changes) was paid by everyone, used by few. We chose six platforms that genuinely matter to SMB and built them deeply.
