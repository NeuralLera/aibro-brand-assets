Honest concessions

Where DIY still wins.

DIY is genuinely better in three situations:

  • You're building a personal brand. Personal voice has to be yours. SmashOne can post your photos and reply to routine DMs, but your audience came for your face — not the AI's voice.
  • You enjoy it. If posting and replying are creative outlets, automation strips the part you love.
  • You have a part-time hire already doing it. A $400/month part-time social manager beats $79 if they're already familiar with your brand and the team works well.
