1. Introduction

SMASHONE CORPORATION (Florida C-Corp, registration P26000023598) operates the SmashOne service available at smashone.us and the dashboard at app.smashone.us. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect about customers, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have.

This policy applies to the SmashOne service for US-resident customers. For our automator program, please also see the Terms of Service for the commercial relationship terms.

2. Data we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account data

Email address, business name, business type (industry), city, and password (hashed). For automators: Stripe Connect identity verification data is held by Stripe, not by us.

Billing data

Payment card information is collected and stored directly by Stripe. We receive only a payment method token and your billing email. We do not store card numbers, CVV codes, or expiration dates.

Connected social account data

When you authorize SmashOne to manage your Facebook Page, Instagram account, Telegram channel, WhatsApp Business profile, TikTok account, or Google Business Profile, we receive access tokens that permit reading and posting to those accounts. We store the tokens encrypted and use them only for the actions you configure.

Content data

Posts you author, schedule, or auto-generate; AI-drafted replies; your business catalog; and customer DMs you receive on connected platforms.

Usage data

Pages visited on smashone.us and app.smashone.us, dashboard actions, errors, and performance telemetry. See section 5 on cookies.

3. How we use data

  • Provide the service you signed up for — scheduled posting, AI DM replies, analytics.
  • Process payments via Stripe.
  • Send transactional emails (billing receipts, security alerts, trial-ending notices).
  • Improve the service through aggregated, de-identified usage analytics.
  • Comply with US law (tax reporting, anti-fraud, lawful subpoenas).
What we do not do: We do not sell your data. We do not train external AI models on your content. We do not allow third parties to access your business catalog or customer DMs except as required to deliver the SmashOne service (e.g., the AI provider that powers DM replies).

4. Sharing & disclosure

We share data with the following categories of service providers, under contract:

  • Stripe — payment processing, Connect identity verification.
  • Social platform APIs (Meta, Telegram, Google, ByteDance) — when you authorize publishing on those platforms.
  • AI infrastructure (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI) — when generating draft posts and DM replies. Prompts are sent under data processing terms that prohibit retention beyond the request.
  • Cloud hosting (DigitalOcean, AWS S3 for backups) — encrypted at rest, US data centers.
  • Email delivery (Postmark, AWS SES) — transactional emails.
  • Analytics (Plausible, self-hosted) — privacy-friendly, no cross-site tracking.

We may disclose data when required by US law, court order, or to protect SmashOne's rights and safety.

5. Cookies & tracking

SmashOne uses two categories of cookies:

  • Essential — session cookies, theme preference (light/dark), CSRF tokens. These are required for the service to function and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics — Plausible analytics (privacy-friendly, no cross-site tracking, no third-party cookies).

See the Cookies Policy for the full list.

6. Data retention

We retain your data while your subscription is active. After cancellation:

  • Account data: retained 90 days (grace period to resume), then anonymized.
  • Content data: exportable as CSV up to 90 days post-cancel, then permanently deleted.
  • Billing records: retained 7 years per US tax law.
  • Connected platform tokens: revoked immediately on cancel.

7. Security

SmashOne uses industry-standard practices:

  • TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest.
  • Stripe handles all payment data (PCI DSS Level 1).
  • SOC 2 Type II audit in progress as of 2026-05-12.
  • Encrypted database backups, US-region only.
  • Annual third-party penetration testing.

No system is perfectly secure. In the unlikely event of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of discovery and report to relevant authorities per US state law.

8. Your rights (CCPA & US state privacy laws)

If you are a California resident, the CCPA grants you the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you.
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements for billing).
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt out of any "sale" or "sharing" — though SmashOne does not sell data.
  • Non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

Submit requests via privacy@smashone.us. We verify identity before fulfilling. Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and other states with similar laws have equivalent rights.

9. Children

SmashOne is intended for business owners 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact privacy@smashone.us and we will delete it.

10. Changes

We will notify customers by email at least 30 days before any material change to this policy. Continued use of the service after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
