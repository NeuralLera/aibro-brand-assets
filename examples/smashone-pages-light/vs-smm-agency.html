Honest concessions

Where an agency still wins.

We won't pretend SmashOne replaces every reason to hire an agency. Here's the truth:

  • Brand strategy + positioning workshops. An agency runs a multi-week discovery and writes you a brand voice doc. SmashOne assumes you already know who you are.
  • Original video and photo production. Agencies shoot. SmashOne posts what you've already shot (your phone gallery counts).
  • Paid ad campaign management. Some agencies run your Meta Ads and Google Ads spend. SmashOne is organic-only — posts and DM replies, not paid media buying.
  • Real-time crisis comms. Bad review going viral? A human PR pro at an agency picks up the phone. SmashOne AI hands off to you for that.
