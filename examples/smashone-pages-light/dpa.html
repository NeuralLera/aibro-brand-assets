1. Scope

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") supplements the SmashOne Terms of Service for business customers who handle personal data of their own customers through the service (e.g., processing direct messages from their followers).

It applies whenever SmashOne (the "Processor") processes personal data on behalf of the customer (the "Controller").

2. Roles

Controller: You — the SmashOne customer — determine the purposes and means of processing your end-customers' data.

Processor: SMASHONE CORPORATION processes that data only on your documented instructions to deliver the service.

3. Data categories

SmashOne processes the following on your behalf:

  • Direct messages and replies to/from your social media followers.
  • Public comments on your posts that the AI assistant references when drafting replies.
  • Your business catalog (services, products, hours) referenced by the AI.
  • Analytics aggregated from your social platform APIs.

4. Processor obligations

SmashOne agrees to:

  • Process personal data only on your documented instructions.
  • Maintain confidentiality with all personnel handling personal data.
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures (section 6).
  • Engage sub-processors only under written terms equivalent to this DPA (section 5).
  • Assist you in responding to data subject requests (section 8).
  • Notify you of personal data breaches without undue delay (section 7).
  • Delete or return personal data after termination (section 9).

6. Security measures

  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest.
  • Access control: Least-privilege; production access logged and audited.
  • Backups: Encrypted, US-region only, retained 30 days.
  • Penetration testing: Annual third-party.
  • SOC 2 Type II: Audit in progress as of 2026-05-12.
  • Vulnerability management: Critical patches within 7 days; high within 30 days.

7. Breach notification

In the event of a personal data breach affecting your data, SmashOne will notify you by email within 72 hours of discovery, including: nature of the breach, categories of data affected, estimated number of records, likely consequences, and measures taken or proposed.

8. Data subject requests

If an end-customer of yours exercises a right (e.g., access, deletion, correction), they should normally direct it to you. SmashOne will assist by providing technical means to locate, export, or delete the relevant data within 30 days of your request.

9. Deletion on termination

Within 90 days of contract termination, SmashOne will delete or return all personal data processed on your behalf, unless retention is required by US law (billing records, audit logs). Deletion of social platform access tokens is immediate.
