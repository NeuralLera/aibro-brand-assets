Honest concessions

Where Hootsuite or Buffer still wins.

If you're a marketing team — not a solo SMB owner — these tools may serve you better:

  • Team approval workflows. Hootsuite's enterprise plans support multi-stage editorial review. SmashOne is single-owner approve-and-post.
  • Broader platform coverage. Hootsuite supports 20+ social platforms breadthwise. SmashOne deliberately ships 6 — the ones SMBs actually need for posting and DM replies.
  • Enterprise reporting suites. Hootsuite Enterprise has Salesforce integration and competitor benchmarking. SmashOne is dashboard-only analytics.
  • Buffer's $15/mo is genuinely cheaper if you only need scheduling and you have someone else replying to DMs. SmashOne $79 reflects the AI-reply layer.
