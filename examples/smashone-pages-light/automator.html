Path 1 · Highest earner

Hustler

FINDS clients · SETS them up

You source the client (your network, cold outreach, content marketing) and configure their SmashOne. You earn on both the setup work and the subscription.

Per-client year-1

$139.30 setup (70% × $199) + $94.80 L1 commission ($79/mo × 12 × 10%) = $234.10 year-1 from one base subscriber.

  • 70% × $199 setup fee per client

Path 3 / 4

By-request setter

SETS up clients others sent

You don't find clients. You wait for SmashOne to match you (Path 4) or a referrer to send one (Path 3). You configure their accounts and earn the setup fee.

Per setup

$99.50 setup (50% × $199). No subscription commission — that goes to SmashOne or the referrer.

  • 50% × $199 setup fee per setup
  • 50% × addon setup fees ($25 / $20 / $75)
  • No subscription L1/L2 commission
  • Best for setup specialists, part-time
  • Lower risk: paid for completed work

  • No setup work required
  • Best for creators, influencers, communities

How L1 and L2 work

Two levels of partnership.

You earn 10% lifetime from customers you bring (Level 1). And 5% lifetime from customers your Level-1 automators bring (Level 2). Caps at L2 — no infinite tier marketing.

If your client cancels Days 1–14 (trial period), the $199 setup fee is never charged, so you receive no setup commission. This is the Hustler risk. From Day 15 onward, the setup fee is charged and your commission posts — but the client can request a money-back on the subscription Days 15–29, which does not claw back your setup commission (the setup fee is non-refundable after Day 15).
No. Each business is on exactly one path. The path is determined at signup by how the client arrived: your referral link (Path 1/2b), a friend's link (Path 2b/3), self-signup (Path 2a), or a setup request (Path 4). If the same client opens a second business, that's a separate signup and can be on a different path.
When a client adds WhatsApp ($29 + $49 setup), TikTok ($19 + $39 setup), or Google Business Profile ($39 + $149 setup), the same path logic applies. Hustler (Path 1): you earn 70% of the addon setup fee + 10% L1 on the monthly addon subscription. By-request setter: 50% of the addon setup fee, no monthly commission. Link-distributor: 10% L1 on the monthly addon subscription.
Commissions post to your Stripe balance after Day 29 (the money-back window closes Day 29 to protect SmashOne from refund chargeback risk). Stripe automatically pays out monthly to your linked bank account. Holiday delays may push payouts by 1–2 business days.
